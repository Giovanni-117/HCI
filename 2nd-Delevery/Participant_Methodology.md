# Participant Methodology

## Overview
This section describes the methodology used to involve participants in the usability research of the system.

## Participant Selection
Participants were selected based on their relevance to the target user groups identified in the first phase of the project. The selection focused on individuals who represent typical users of the system.

## Sample Size
A small representative sample of users was selected to participate in usability activities. This sample size allows the team to identify the most common usability problems efficiently.

## Participation Methods
Participants will contribute through the following activities:

- Interviews to understand their needs and expectations.
- Usability testing sessions to evaluate the ease of use of the system.
- Short surveys to gather feedback about their experience.

## Ethical Considerations
All participants will be informed about the purpose of the study and their participation will be voluntary. Any collected information will be used only for research purposes.

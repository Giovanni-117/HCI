# 📝 Reflection: The Importance of Nielsen's Heuristics

---

### **Overview**
Jakob Nielsen created **ten rules** for design in 1990, and they are still very important today. Technology changes fast, but the way humans think and see things stays the same. These heuristics are not just about making things look "pretty"; they are about making tools that are easy to use.

### **Key Benefits**
* **User Experience:** When designers follow these rules, they help users avoid mistakes and frustration.
* **Cost Efficiency:** This saves a lot of money for companies because it is easier to fix a problem during the design phase than after the product is finished.
* **Future-Proof:** Even with new technology like AI, these principles help us stay in control of our digital tools.

### **Conclusion**
Finally, we should think about adding a new rule for **accessibility**, so everyone can use technology without problems.

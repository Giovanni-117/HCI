
### The Enduring Value of Nielsen's Heuristics
> Despite rapid technological changes, the ten usability rules created by Jakob Nielsen in 1990 remain the fundamental pillar for designing any modern digital system.
> This is because, while machines evolve at breakneck speed, the way the human brain processes information remains exactly the same. Rather than just being simple visual tips,
> these rules are the true foundation that helps creators build user-friendly tools that prevent frustration, allow us to easily fix mistakes, and save companies significant money
> by catching logical flaws before they reach the public. Even in the era of artificial intelligence, these principles adapt perfectly to keep technology under our control, proving
> that their only real pending task today is the addition of a mandatory eleventh rule on universal accessibility so the digital world can be truly inclusive for everyone.
